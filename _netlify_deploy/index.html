Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about NaijaMall

How does the escrow payment system work?

+

Our escrow system protects both buyers and sellers. When you make a purchase, your payment is securely held by NaijaMall. The funds are only released to the seller after you confirm receipt and satisfaction with your order. The escrow fee is ‚Ç¶500 per transaction.

What areas do you deliver to?

+

We currently provide same-day delivery service across Lagos State, including areas like Lekki, Victoria Island, Ikeja, Surulere, and more. We're constantly expanding to new locations.

What is the market procurement fee?

+

The market procurement fee ranges from ‚Ç¶1,500 to ‚Ç¶5,000 depending on your shopping amount. This covers the cost of our agents shopping on your behalf at the rural markets.

How long does delivery take?

+

We offer same-day delivery for orders placed before 12 PM. Orders placed after 5PM will be delivered the next business day. You'll receive real-time tracking updates from our verified riders.

How do I become a seller on NaijaMall?

+

Click on "Become a Seller" and fill out the registration form. You'll need to provide your business details, valid ID, and bank information. Our team will verify your details within 24-48 hours. Sellers pay a 5% commission on sales from our platform.

What payment methods do you accept?

+

We accept all major payment methods including bank transfers, debit cards, USSD, and mobile money. All payments are processed securely through our escrow system.

Can I return products if I'm not satisfied?

+

Yes! If you're not satisfied with your order, please complain within 30mins of item delivery. Since your payment is held in escrow, you won't be charged until you confirm satisfaction.

How fresh are the products?

+

All our products come directly from rural markets and farms, ensuring maximum freshness. Items are procured on the same day or day before delivery to guarantee quality.

What are the fees for riders?

+

Riders earn competitive rates with a 10% platform fee per delivery. Join our rider network to start earning today!

How can I track my order?

+

Once your order is confirmed, you'll receive real-time tracking updates via SMS and email. You can also track your order directly on the website using your order number.
