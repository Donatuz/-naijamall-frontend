## For Sellers: How to Get Started

### Step 1: Register as a Seller
1. Go to the NaijaMall homepage
2. Click **"Register"** button
3. Fill in your details:
   - **First Name** and **Last Name**
   - **Email Address** (for login and notifications)
   - **Phone Number** (Nigerian format: +234...)
   - **Account Type:** Select **"Seller"**
   - **Business Name** ‚≠ê (This will appear when you select Seller)
   - **Business Description** (Optional - tell buyers about your business)
   - **Password** (minimum 6 characters)
   - **Address** (Your shop/business location)
4. Check "I agree to Terms & Conditions"
5. Click **"Create Account"**
6. You'll be automatically logged in and redirected to your dashboard!

### Step 2: Access Your Seller Dashboard
After registration, you'll see:
- **Welcome message** with your name and business
- **Verification badge** (status: Pending/Verified/Rejected)
- **Statistics:** Total Products, Active Products, Sales, Orders
- **"Add New Product"** button
- **Your Products** section (empty at first)

‚ö†Ô∏è **Important:** Your account will be "Pending" verification initially. You'll see a notification about this.

### Step 3: Wait for Verification (Admin Required)
- New sellers need admin verification before adding products
- This is a one-time process for security
- Contact the admin to verify your account
- Once verified, the badge will turn green ‚úÖ

### Step 4: Add Your First Product
1. Click **"Add New Product"** on your dashboard
2. Fill in **Basic Information:**
   - Product Name (e.g., "Fresh Tomatoes")
   - Description (detailed info about your product)
   - Category (select from dropdown)
   - Tags (comma-separated, e.g., "fresh, organic, local")

3. Fill in **Pricing & Stock:**
   - Price in Naira (‚Ç¶)
   - Unit (kg, piece, bundle, etc.)
   - Stock Quantity (how many you have)
   - Discount % (optional)

4. Fill in **Product Details:**
   - ‚úì Check if it's a fresh/perishable product
   - Add Market Location (optional)

5. **Upload Images** (up to 5):
   - **Drag & drop** images into the upload area, OR
   - **Click** the upload area to browse files
   - Each image max 5MB
   - Supported: JPG, PNG
   - You'll see previews with √ó button to remove

6. Click **"Add Product"**
7. Success! You'll be redirected back to your dashboard

### Step 5: Manage Your Products
From your dashboard, each product card shows:
- Product image
- Name and price
- Stock status
- Active/Inactive status

**Actions you can take:**
- **Edit:** Update product details and images
- **Enable/Disable:** Toggle product availability (without deleting)
- **Delete:** Remove product permanently (asks for confirmation)

### Step 6: Update or Edit Products
1. Click **"Edit"** on any product
2. Form will be pre-filled with current data
3. Make your changes
4. Add/remove images as needed
5. Click **"Update Product"**

## üÜò Need Help?

### Common Issues

**Q: I can't add products**
A: Your account needs to be verified by admin first. Check your verification status badge on the dashboard.

**Q: Images won't upload**
A: Make sure:
- Image is JPG or PNG format
- Image size is under 5MB
- You're uploading max 5 images per product

**Q: I forgot my password**
A: Use the "Forgot Password" link on the login page (if implemented)

**Q: How do I change my business name?**
A: Go to Profile Settings from the user menu

**Q: Where do I see my orders?**
A: Click your name in the top menu ‚Üí "My Orders"
